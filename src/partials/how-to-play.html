How to play

  • Match elements

    Line up three or more similar items to clear the board and earn resources.

  • Solve puzzles

    Each level presents unique challenges with limited moves. Plan your strategies wisely!

  • Build and decorate

    Use resources from puzzles to build structures, beautify your island, and unlock new areas.

  • Complete quests

    Engage in various quests and challenges to earn extra rewards and boost your progress.
